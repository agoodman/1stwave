What is FirstWave Venture Center?

We are a 16,000 sq ft innovation center positioned in the heart of downtown Tampa. We help developers, entrepreneurs, designers, tech startups, business mentors and more communicate, collaborate, and innovate their ideas and businesses.

Operated by Tampa Bay WaVE - a nonprofit dedicated to helping entrepreneurs build, launch and grow thriving tech ventures in Tampa Bay - the FirstWaVE Venture Center is home to several tech startups as well as a whole host of other entrepreneurial support organizations too, like Florida Next, Startup Weekend Tampa, Startup Bus Southeast, and more.

Come visit us on the second floor of Rivergate Tower (aka, the "Sykes building") for one of our many events or to cowork with the best and brightest from our local tech community. See you soon!
