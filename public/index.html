By Entrepreneurs, For Entrepreneurs

TampaBayWaVE is an 'by entrepreneur, for entrepreneur' non-profit that helps entrepreneurs turn ideas into growing tech ventures in Tampa Bay.

What is FirstWave Venture Center?

We are a 16,000 sq ft innovation center positioned in the heart of downtown Tampa. We help designers, developers, entrepreneurs, and tech startups communicate, collaborate, and build their ideas and businesses. Come visit us on the second floor of Rivergate Tower (Sykes building) for one of our many events or to cowork with the best and brightest community leaders. See you soon!
